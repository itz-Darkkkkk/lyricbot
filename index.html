🎵 Welcome to Lyric Bot

Lyric Bot is a powerful, secure, and intelligent Discord bot designed to enhance your server experience. Whether you're managing a study community, hosting stage events, or just vibing with friends, Lyric brings moderation, utility, and entertainment—all in one elegant package.

✨ Why Choose Lyric?

  • Voice Recording: Capture entire VC or Stage sessions and automatically send recordings to a configured channel.
  • Moderation Aware: Detects violations (e.g. no camera or screenshare in required channels) and auto-warns or disconnects users.
  • UltraCoins Economy: Unique reward system encouraging productivity and interaction.
  • To-Do Manager: With cool-down and role-based restrictions for fair task logging.
  • Rich Embed Support: Professional announcements, reports, and logs.
  • Admin-Only Commands: Controlled access by user ID and role ID.

🛡️ Security & Compliance

Lyric follows best practices in data protection, encryption, and platform compliance (GDPR, CCPA). Data collected (like user IDs or timestamps) is stored securely and never shared.
